FAQ

GOT
QUESTIONS?

Is CaliGo really free?
Yes — CaliGo's core features are completely free: workout logging, skill progressions, and the exercise library. We're building a premium tier for advanced analytics and coaching, but the fundamentals will always be free.
Do I need any equipment?
Not necessarily. CaliGo has full programs for zero equipment, but also programs leveraging a pull-up bar, rings, or parallel bars. You choose your setup and the app adapts.
I'm a complete beginner. Is this for me?
Absolutely. CaliGo was built with beginners in mind. Every skill has entry points that require zero strength — we'll build you up safely and progressively. The first workout literally starts with learning how to breathe correctly.
How is CaliGo different from YouTube tutorials?
YouTube gives you content. CaliGo gives you a system. Random videos don't track your progress, adjust to your recovery, or tell you when you're ready for the next step. CaliGo connects your sessions into a structured, evolving training journey.
What platform is CaliGo available on?
CaliGo is a web app — it works on any browser on your phone, tablet, or desktop. No download required. Mobile-native apps are on the roadmap.
